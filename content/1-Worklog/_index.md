## Worklog

This worklog records my internship in the **First Cloud Journey (FCAJ)** program, carried out over **12 weeks (about 3 months)**, from **21/04/2025 to 10/07/2025**.

The program was split into two phases:

- **Weeks 1-8 — Learning AWS:** I built cloud fundamentals and hands-on skills with the core AWS services through the [Cloud Journey](https://cloudjourney.awsstudygroup.com/) workshops and the [FCJ Bootcamp](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLahN4TLWtox2a3vElknwzU_urND8hLn1i) playlist, completing a lab for almost every topic (IAM, EC2, VPC, S3/CloudFront, DynamoDB/Lambda, EventBridge/Step Functions, CloudWatch...).
- **Weeks 9-12 — Capstone project:** a *Meeting Recording & AI Task-Assignment* system on AWS. My role focused on **researching and selecting the right services, estimating monthly cost, evaluating architectural suitability, and assessing real-world applicability for businesses.**
